Capture beautiful, pixel-perfect element screenshots.

A lightweight, reliable Chrome extension that preserves the page's original styling. No broken layouts, just what you see.

Add to Chrome Documentation

Designed for details.

Pixel-Perfect, Every Time

Unlike tools that re-render HTML, Element Snap captures the actual pixels on your screen. Fonts, shadows, and complex CSS are preserved exactly as they appear.

Privacy First

Zero tracking. Zero external requests. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Smart Stitching

Automatically detects and stitches scrolling elements into a single seamless image.

Theme Aware

Interface automatically adapts to your system's Light or Dark mode preference.

Padding Controls

Add breathable whitespace around your captures with real-time preview.
